Husky

The Remarkable History of Huskies as Working Dogs - Otto ...Siberian Husky history begins over 4,000 years ago with the Chukchi people of Siberia, who developed the breed as resilient sled dogs for transportation and companionship in harsh Arctic conditions, capable of pulling light loads long distances.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a popular medium-large dog breed known for its gentle nature, intelligence, and beautiful golden coat, making it an excellent family companion and versatile working dog.

Pug

Pugs originated in ancient China over 2,000 years ago as cherished companions for emperors and nobility, valued for luck and luxury, evolving from "lion dogs" to lapdogs, then spreading to Europe via Dutch traders where they became favorites of royalty like Queen Victoria and Napoleon's wife Josephine, later gaining AKC recognition in the US in 1885.

Bulldog

The Bulldog, often called the English or British Bulldog, is a medium-sized breed recognized for its "sourmug" face and stocky, low-slung build. Originally a fierce athlete in blood sports, modern Bulldogs have been bred to be gentle, courageous companions.
